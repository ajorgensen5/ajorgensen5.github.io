Tune-up Packages

We offer free inspections on any bicycle. If you're not sure what's wrong, bring your bike to us. We'll put it on the stand, figure out exactly what your machine needs to run smoothly again, and give you a complete estimate before we do any work.

Silver Tune-up Package

Includes adjustment of your shifting & braking systems, tire inflation, a complete functionality and safety inspection, and (of course) chain lubrication.

Gold Package

Includes the Silver package, plus: Shifting adjustment and component lubrication, lateral wheel truing, complete bearing inspection and adjustment, and a light frame cleaning.

Platinum Overhaul Package

Includes the Gold package, plus:

Cable and housing replacement,removal and thorough cleaning of drivetrain components, including the crankset, chain, rear derailleur and cassette.
