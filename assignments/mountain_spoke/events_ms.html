Cycle the Continental Divide

  • One Stage: 2745mi/4418km
  • Self-Supported Racing
  • Great Devide Mountain Bike Route
  • 15th of June 2018

Cycle the Continental Divide is a (CCD) is a long-distance, off-road bicycle touring route from Banff, Alberta, Canada to Antelope Wells, New Mexico, USA. As of 2016, the route is 2768.4 miles (4455.3 km) long. A set of highly detailed route maps and a guidebook are available from Adventure Cycling.
